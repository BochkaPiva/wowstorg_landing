# WOWSTORG Design Direction

## Design Read

Premium dark brand landing for a Russian event agency. The visual language is cinematic, minimal, and physical: a black stage, one memorable mascot asset, restrained typography, clean navigation, and a clear request path.

This must not look like a generic AI landing page. It should feel designed, edited, and intentional.

## Design Dials

- Design variance: 7
- Motion intensity: 4
- Visual density: 3

Meaning:

- expressive composition is allowed;
- motion should be calm and premium;
- every fold should have one dominant idea;
- avoid dense card grids and decorative filler.

## Visual Principles

- True black or near-black is the primary stage.
- One accent color: WOWSTORG yellow.
- The dinosaur video is the main brand asset, not a side decoration.
- Use negative space as a premium material.
- Keep copy short on first-screen sections.
- Use cards only when they represent real objects, catalog items, steps, or repeated content.
- Do not use generic glassmorphism, random gradients, animated blobs, or decorative grid backgrounds.

## Typography

Typography should feel strong, contemporary, and expensive.

Rules:

- use display sans for major headlines;
- use restrained body sizing with comfortable line-height on dark backgrounds;
- avoid tiny uppercase labels above every section;
- avoid gradient text;
- avoid over-tight letter spacing;
- headings should stay readable on mobile and never overflow.

Future font direction:

- keep a strong grotesque display face for hero and section headlines;
- pair with a highly readable sans for body;
- self-host fonts before production.

## Hero

Hero requirements:

- black background;
- centered dinosaur video;
- video must blend into the section with bottom gradient/mask;
- menu is transparent, compact, and premium;
- top-right CTA stays visible;
- bottom-left SEO text stays short and elegant;
- no heavy text block covering the dinosaur;
- no scroll cue unless it serves a clear purpose.

## Motion

Motion should support hierarchy and tactility:

- soft hover transitions;
- restrained button feedback;
- gentle reveal timing;
- no constant decorative movement beyond the video itself;
- reduced-motion support must keep content visible.

## SEO And Content

SEO is part of the information architecture.

Required page logic:

1. Hero with clear event-agency positioning.
2. Sharp statement about business tasks becoming game experiences.
3. Event formats.
4. Catalog preview for props, games, services, and packages.
5. Experience mechanic: how guests move through the event.
6. Scenarios and future cases.
7. Production confidence.
8. FAQ with real commercial questions.
9. Final CTA.

Catalog route now present:

- `/catalog`

Future SEO routes after real catalog content is published:

## Quality Bar

Before considering a visual iteration done:

- desktop and mobile hero must look composed;
- CTA labels must not wrap awkwardly;
- text contrast must be readable;
- the page must build successfully;
- visible Russian copy must be natural;
- no fake reviews, fake logos, fake ratings, or fake structured data;
- no agent or skill folders should be committed.
