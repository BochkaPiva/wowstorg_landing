# Landing Structure

## Design Read

ВАУСТОРГ should feel like a premium experiential/event agency with a playful mascot,
not a childish entertainment vendor and not a cold corporate contractor.

The page must combine:

- cinematic first impression;
- clear event formats;
- catalog of props and services;
- proof through scenarios and later real cases;
- safe, serious production process;
- a focused request flow.

## Recommended Page Flow

1. Hero: cinematic dinosaur, black stage, minimal copy, primary CTA in navigation.
2. Positioning: one sharp statement about turning business tasks into game events.
3. Event formats: corporate events, team buildings, interactive zones, festivals, exhibitions.
4. Catalog preview: props, games, packages, branded mechanics, rental.
5. Wow mechanic: how a guest moves through the experience.
6. Scenario/cases: where it works, with task/solution/effect.
7. Production confidence: how the team prepares mechanics, logistics, safety, timing.
8. FAQ: budget, deadlines, venue, guest count, customization, rental.
9. Final CTA: describe the task and get a format proposal.

## Catalog Placement

The catalog should appear before cases. Reason: clients often need to understand
what can be ordered before they believe the scenarios.

Recommended catalog entry points:

- top navigation item: “Каталог”;
- section after formats;
- CTA text: “Получить подборку игр”;
- future separate SEO routes for category pages.

## Conversion Logic

The page should not push “contact us” too early. The better promise is:

> Опишите задачу, а мы предложим формат и подборку игр под площадку, бюджет и аудиторию.

This turns the CTA into a useful next step instead of a sales request.
